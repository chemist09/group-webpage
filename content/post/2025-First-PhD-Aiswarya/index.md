We are delighted to welcome Aiswarya Rajeev as the first doctoral researcher in the Biomolecular Systems and Design Lab. Her project focuses on the membrane activity of self-assembling peptide fibrils, investigating how peptide sequence and self-assembly state govern interactions with lipid membranes and biological activity.

Her work combines biophysical experiments including QCM-D and fluorescence spectroscopy with molecular dynamics simulations, and connects to both disease-relevant questions in amyloid biology and the design of peptide materials.
