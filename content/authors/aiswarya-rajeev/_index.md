Aiswarya Rajeev joined the Biomolecular Systems and Design Lab in 2025 as the first doctoral researcher. Her project investigates how self-assembling peptides interact with lipid membranes, combining QCM-D, fluorescence spectroscopy, and molecular dynamics simulations to understand how peptide sequence and aggregation state govern membrane activity and selectivity.
