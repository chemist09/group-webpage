This privacy notice explains how personal data is handled when you use this website (johnlab.de).

**Data Collection**

When you visit this website, no personal data is actively collected by the website owner, Torsten John. Your personal information, such as your name or email address, is neither requested nor stored during your interaction with this website.

**Hosting**

This website is hosted by Netlify, Inc. (101 2nd Street, San Francisco, CA 94105, USA). When you access this website, your browser transmits certain technical data to Netlify's servers, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, and the pages accessed. This data may be stored in server logs by Netlify. Netlify may process this data on servers located outside the European Union. For more information, see Netlify's privacy policy: [https://www.netlify.com/privacy/](https://www.netlify.com/privacy/)

The domain johnlab.de is registered with STRATO GmbH (Otto-Ostrowski-Straße 7, 10249 Berlin, Germany), which provides DNS services only and does not process visitor data beyond domain resolution.

**Tracking Tools**

This website does not utilise any tracking tools, analytics services, or cookies. Your browsing activity on this website remains anonymous to the website owner.

**External Services**

The Contact page displays a map provided by OpenStreetMap via Mapnik tile servers. When you load the Contact page, your browser requests map tiles from OpenStreetMap servers, which may result in your IP address being transmitted to the OpenStreetMap Foundation. For more information, see OpenStreetMap's privacy policy: [https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy](https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy)

This website uses system-native fonts and does not load fonts from external servers.

**Data Usage and Sharing**

As no personal data is actively collected by the website owner, there is no usage or sharing of your personal information. Any information you provide voluntarily through other means of communication, such as email correspondence, will be treated confidentially and in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

**Your Rights**

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to access, rectify, or delete any personal data that may have been collected, as well as the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. As this website does not actively collect personal data, these rights apply primarily to data processed by the hosting provider (Netlify) as described above.
