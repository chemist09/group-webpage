title: Join the Lab
---

The Biomolecular Systems and Design Lab welcomes motivated researchers and students interested in peptide self-assembly, biomolecular interfaces, and computational biophysics. Our projects combine experimental and computational approaches. Prior experience in all methods is not required. We value curiosity, rigour, and willingness to learn.

---

### Postdoctoral Researchers

We welcome enquiries from postdoctoral researchers interested in joining the lab on an independent fellowship. We actively support applications to competitive fellowship schemes, including:

- [Humboldt Research Fellowship, Alexander von Humboldt Foundation](https://www.humboldt-foundation.de/en/apply/sponsorship-programmes/humboldt-research-fellowship) for international scholars
- [Marie Skłodowska-Curie Postdoctoral Fellowship, EU](https://marie-sklodowska-curie-actions.ec.europa.eu/actions/postdoctoral-fellowships) for international scholars
- [Walter Benjamin Programme, DFG](https://www.dfg.de/en/research-funding/funding-opportunities/programmes/individual/walter-benjamin)
- [DAAD Fellowships](https://www.daad.de/en/studying-in-germany/scholarships/) for international scholars

If you are interested in applying for a fellowship to work in our lab, please get in touch early, so we can develop a competitive proposal together.

---

### PhD Students

We are always interested in hearing from prospective doctoral researchers. Funded positions will be advertised below when available. Prospective students are also strongly encouraged to apply for external doctoral fellowships. We actively support such applications. Relevant schemes include:

- [Stipendium plus](https://stipendiumplus.de/promovierende/) (Studienstiftung, Friedrich-Ebert-Stiftung, and others)
- [DAAD Fellowships](https://www2.daad.de/deutschland/stipendium/datenbank/en/21148-scholarship-database/) for international students

A strong application typically includes a background in chemistry, biochemistry, biophysics, materials science, or a related discipline. Experience with experimental or computational methods is a plus but not required. We provide training in all techniques used in the lab.

---

### Master and Bachelor Students

We host M.Sc. and B.Sc. thesis students from Constructor University and other institutions. Projects are available in both experimental and computational directions. If you are interested in a thesis project, please get in touch with a brief description of your interests.
