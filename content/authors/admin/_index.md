Torsten John is Assistant Professor of Physical Chemistry at Constructor University in Bremen, where he leads a research group in Biophysical and Computational Chemistry. His work focuses on peptide self-assembly and interactions at interfaces, combining experimental and computational approaches to explore fundamental mechanisms and develop functional materials. He earned his PhD in chemistry with _summa cum laude_ from Leipzig University and conducted research at several international institutions including the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), Monash University, and the Max Planck Institute for Polymer Research.
