Our QSense Omni 2 QCM-D instrument from Biolin Scientific AB has been delivered and installed in our wet laboratory. The instrument will support our research on biomolecular interactions, lipid membranes, and peptide-membrane activity studies. We thank Constructor University for the support and Quantum Design Europe for installation.
